About Dashboard
The COVID-risk dashboard looks at data to understand hyper-local risk due to COVID-19 on Pune city and supplements it with micronarratives from doctors.

It combines COVID hazard, healthcare infrastructure capacity, population vulnerabilities and expert narratives to arrive at a multidimensional risk assessment for Pune city.

For further details, please refer to the report Stories and Statistics: A multidimensional risk dashboard for COVID-19
